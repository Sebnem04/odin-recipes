"Plov"

Description

Azerbaijani Plov: Fragrant rice with tender meat, veggies, and spices.

Pure delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 lb (225g) lamb/beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • Spices: cumin, turmeric
  • Salt, pepper
  • 2 1/2 cups water/broth

Steps

  1. Sauté onions, brown meat in oil.
  2. Add spices, rice, carrots. Sauté.
  3. Pour water/broth. Boil, then simmer covered for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Fluff, serve—a taste of Azerbaijani tradition.
