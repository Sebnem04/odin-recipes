"Omelette"

Description

Omelette: Quick, simple, and versatile.

A delicious way to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter or oil
  • Fillings (cheese, veggies, etc.)

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, add pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter/oil in pan.
  3. Pour eggs, cook until set.
  4. Add fillings, fold, cook briefly.
